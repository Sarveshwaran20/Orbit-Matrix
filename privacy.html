Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Orbit Matrix ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application accesses, uses, and handles your data when you use our service. Orbit Matrix operates entirely as a client-side application running directly within your web browser.

2. Information We Access

When you sign in using your Google Account, Orbit Matrix requests authorization to access specific Google APIs. Specifically, we request access to:

  • Google Drive API: To allow you to search, view, and organize your documents, spreadsheets, and presentations on the canvas.
  • Google Calendar API: To display your upcoming events within the application's sidebar.

3. How We Use the Information

The data accessed through these APIs is used strictly to provide the core functionality of Orbit Matrix. Your files and calendar events are rendered visually within your browser session to create your spatial workspace. We do not use your data for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage and Processing

Orbit Matrix is a zero-backend application. This means:

  • We do not own, operate, or maintain any database servers.
  • We do not transmit your Google user data, files, or calendar events to any external servers.
  • All data processing, including local AI document summarization, occurs offline within your device's browser memory.
  • Your workspace state is saved locally in your own browser's LocalStorage.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Because we do not collect or store your data on external servers, we do not share, sell, or transfer your Google user data to any third parties, advertising networks, or external analytics providers.

6. Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

Orbit Matrix's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Revoking Access

You can revoke Orbit Matrix's access to your Google Account at any time by visiting your Google Account Security settings page and removing Orbit Matrix from the list of apps with access to your account.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository associated with this application.
